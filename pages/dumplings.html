Dumplings

Growing up, I would complain about eating dumplings everyday, even though we barely had it. Now that I am a bit older, I feel the nostalgia when I eat dumplings, making it a bit more enjoyable again.

The special thing about dumplings is that they are very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Meat filling of your choice

Procedure

  1. Make Flour
  2. Make Meat Filling
  3. Boil or Fry the dumplings

Enjoy your dumplings
