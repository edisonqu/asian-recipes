Fuzhou Wonton

A common comfort food and one that my dad loves to create is wontons. These dumpling like meat balls in soup are very tasty and easy to make.

Tasty Chinese Wontons

Ingredients

  • Frozen Chinese Wontons from Walmart
  • Something to heat up

Procedures

  1. Take out the frozen Wontons from Walmart.
  2. Put it in a cup
  3. Pour water in it
  4. Heat it up somehow, anyway is fine (boiling or microwaving)

Enjoy your "Fuzhou" Wontons
